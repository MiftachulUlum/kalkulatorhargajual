Product Price Calculator

Calculate your product's selling price by entering production costs and desired profit margin. It's easy, quick, and accurate!


Formula for Selling Price:

Selling Price = Production Cost + Profit Margin

Steps:
  • 1. Calculate Production Cost:
    This includes costs like materials, labor, and overhead.
  • 2. Determine Profit Margin:
    Set a percentage margin to add as your profit.
  • 3. Calculate Selling Price:
    Add the production cost and profit margin to get the final price.
